This email is to inform you that we have successfully received your request to become a member of ECSA. Thank you!

We will process your request in the next couple of days and get back to you shortly.

In the meantime, you can read the benefits that come with being an ECSA member:

  • share your news and events through our website and newsletter
  • access to members-only internal mailing list for information, job offers, grant application participation requests etc.
  • participate in and vote at our general assembly
  • the chance to be elected to our executive board
  • participating in ECSA working groups
  • contributing to ECSA documents, such as our policy papers and guidelines
  • invitations to join European citizen science projects
  • contributing to the development of open-access resources for the field, e.g. guides
  • carrying out joint European Citizen Science projects (planned)

ECSA membership is valid for one calendar year, and we will send you an invoice at the start of each year.

If you are eligible to pay the reduced membership fee because you are a student, unemployed or retired or because you are a member of CSA or ACSA, please send proof of this to ecsa-admin@mfn.berlin. If you are eligible to get both reductions remember to please send proof of both.

We are excited that you have decided to join ECSA and look forward to working together.

With kind regards,
